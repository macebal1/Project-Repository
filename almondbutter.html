Almond Butter

How to Make Almond Butter

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Bag Raw Almonds (around 5 cups)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (177°C)

Step 2: Place the almonds on a large tray, and once the oven is hot, place them in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until they have turned golden

Step 3: Transfer the almonds carefully into a food processor and start blending on high speed

Step 4: Stop occasionally to clean the borders of the processor

Step 5: Nuts take time to release their own oils, so pay attention to when the mixture starts to blend and become butter

Step 6: Once the desired consistency is achieved, transfer your butter into a container with lid and don’t cover until the mixture is cooled

Step 7: Store in the fridge
