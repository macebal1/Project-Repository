Lemon Water

How to Make Lemon Water

Ingredients

  • 1 Lemon
  • 8-10 oz. Water

Instructions

Step 1: Wash your lemon

Step 2: Cut out the corner of the lemon with a sharp knife, and slice it in half

Step 3: Place the entire lemon (keep out the corners you cut) on the blender with the water

Step 4: Blend until smooth

Step 5: With a strainer, transfer the lemon water into a glass
