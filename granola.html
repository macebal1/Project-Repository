How to Make Home Made Granola

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Rolled Oats
  • 1 Cup Almonds
  • 1/2 Cup Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1 Teaspoon Sea Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Powdered Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Cup Coconut Oil
  • 1/2 Cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Cup Cranberries (Optional)

Instructions

Step 2: Prepare a baking tray with parchment paper and set aside

Step 3: In a medium bowl mix the rolled oats, almonds, pumpkin seeds, cranberries, salt, and cinnamon

Step 4: In another bowl combine well the coconut oil, honey, and vanilla

Step 5: Slowly transfer the dry ingredients from the first bowl to the second bowl and mix until all ingredients are covered with the honey, coconut oil, and vanilla mixture

Step 6: Transfer the mixture into the tray and spread with a spatula

Step 7: Bake for about 20 minutes until the mixture is golden

Step 8: Let cool completely
