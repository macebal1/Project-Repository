    Some Workout Tips
  • Try to do cardio on an empty stomach. This does not necessarily work for everyone, but it has been very promising for our results.
  • Every morning drink 8oz of lemon water freshly made
  • If desired, take amino acids during training for muscle recovery
  • Listen to your body. If you are tired it might need some rest, but never confuse that with the power of your mind.
  • We trust the results of HIIT: High Intensity Interval Training. So try to incorporate this in your workouts.
  • ALWAYS warm up your body – either with a short jog, 5 minutes of jump rope, or even a warm-up routine
  • Don’t let your routines get boring – change the moves, the weight, even the effort
  • Get a workout partner with your same intentions for extra motivation
