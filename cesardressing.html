Keto Cesar Dressing

How to Make Keto Cesar Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon Cappers
  • Juice of 2 Lemons
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Water (optional for consistency)

Instructions

Step 1: Place all ingredients in a blender (or Nutribullet)

Step 2: Let blend for a while until all ingredients have completely mixed together

Step 3: If too thick, add drops of water and continue to blend until you reach desired consistency

Step 4: Store in a container with lid
