Dark Chocolate Almond Clusters

How to Make Dark Chocolate Almond Clusters

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 Cups Sliced/Crushed Almonds
  • 1/3 Cup Almond Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Coconut Oil
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar-Free Chocolate Chips (Could also be dark chocolate bar chopped in small pieces)
  • 1 Tablespoon Chia Seeds

Instructions

Step 1: Cover a large plate or board with parchment paper and place it in the freezer while you prepare the batter

Step 2: In a bowl (microwave save) add the almond butter, coconut oil, and sugar-free chocolate chips.

Step 3: Microwave the ingredients. However, every 30 seconds stir the mix to avoid the almond butter or chocolate from burning. The mixture should be fully combined and melted. If you don’t have access to a microwave, place all the ingredients in a small saucepan under medium heat and stir at all times until the chocolate melts and the ingredients are fully combined

Step 4: Transfer the sliced almonds and chia seeds into the mixture making sure that the almonds and the seeds are fully covered with the mixture

Step 5: Remove the plate from the freezer

Step 6: Spoon the batter with the size of the cookie desired and place it on the cold plate (on top of the parchment paper to avoid sticking). Since the plate is cold, the cookie should not expand too much and the bottom part should set pretty fast on the plate. Leave some space between each cookie as a preventative measure. Repeat this process until there is no more batter left.

Step 7: Place the plate with the cookies in the freezer for about 10 minutes or when you see that the chocolate has set and is firm

Step 8: Store the cookies in an air tight container in the fridge so that they are crunchy
