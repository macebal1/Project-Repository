Seed Crackers

How to Make Seed Crackers

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Chia Seeds
  • 3/4 Cups of Water
  • 2/3 Cups Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1/3 Cup Sesame Seeds (Black)
  • 1/4 Cup Sesame Seeds (White)
  • 1/4 Cup Flax Seeds OR Flax Meal
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 325째F (165째C)

Step 2: In a medium bowl, add the chia seeds and the water. Mix them well and let sit for about 5 minutes. A gelatinous texture should be reached

Step 3: In another large bowl, add the pumpkin seeds, sesame seeds (black and white), flax seeds, salt and pepper and mix them well

Step 4: Transfer the chia seed gelatin mixture into the large bowl and mix all ingredients well

Step 5: Then, transfer the mixture onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet with a spatula and spread it into a thin even layer

Step 6: Bake them for 30 minutes and remove them from the oven

Step 7: Cut into the desired shape of your crackers (squared recommended) and carefully flip each cracker

Step 8: After flipping, bake for an additional 25 minutes

Step 9: Remove from the oven and allow the crackers to cool for around 10 minutes
